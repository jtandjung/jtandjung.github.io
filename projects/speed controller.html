Closed Loop Motor Speed Controller

Summary:
This was the final project of a project/lab course meant to teach students about electronics design through hands-on circuit construction and data acquisition and analysis using the Digilent AD2 (a USB oscilloscope, logic analyzer, and multi-function instrument). This project was centered around building and understanding an optomechanical circuit that can actively control the speed of a servomotor. The project consisted of breadboarding the circuit and investigating the behavior and purpose of each major subcircuit. As part of the final grade, a wire pull test is conducted where the instructor moves a random wire on the breadboard and the student must identify which wire was changed and correct the circuit.
