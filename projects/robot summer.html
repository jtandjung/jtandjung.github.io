Robot Summer: Mario Kart Edition

Summary: Built a robot capable of autonomously navigating a tape "racetrack" in a team of 4. Responsible for design, fabrication, and integration of all onboard electrical systems. Advised on chassis design and performed 3D printing design optimizations on critical mechanical components.
Mechanical Contributions:
  • Optimized 3D printing of wheel mounts to withstand acute mechanical shock
  • Designed and incorporated a supporting backbone column into robot design
  • Fabricated front bumper with shading to stabilize light levels for improved IR light tape tracking
Electrical Contributions:
  • Planned power distribution and wiring for 10+ electrical subsytems in limited space
  • Soldered and tested compact H-bridge and IR beacon detection circuits
  • Designed and created custom high-power reflectance sensor modules capable of 20x greater effective sensing distance than provided pre-packaged sensors
  • Incorporated dual servo motor steering system for responsive steering that was pivotal to enabling autonomous steering of the robot
Final Results:
  • Capable of jumping from a ramp 1 ft high into pebble field
  • Capable of withstanding collisions from other robots and terrain
  • Designed and built from scratch within 6 weeks
  • Consistently finished laps in less than 15 seconds
